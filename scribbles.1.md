# 🏛️ The Personas

## 👑 The Gamemaster

- **Role:** The Executive. Time, Turns, Causality.
- **Logic:** `src/js/gamemaster/` (Director, Loop).
- **UI:** _Phase Indicators, Turn Counters._

Orchestrates the "Director" (Logic) and "Session" (State). Master of Time (Turns), Flow (Orchestration), and Reality (State). Handles the "Game Loop".

## 📜 The Scholar

- **Role:** The Keeper. History, Lore, Research, Definitions, Data, Truth.
- **Logic:** `src/js/scholar/` (DB, Archivist).
- **UI:** _Character Sheets, Quest Logs, Encyclopedias._

## 🛡️ The Warden

- **Role:** The Judge. Laws, Rules, Safety, Security.
- **Logic:** `src/js/warden/` (Reflexes, Laws, Security).
- **UI:** _Debug Console, Settings Panels, Permission Modals._

## 🎭 The Mesmer

- **Role:** The Illusionist. Master of Light, Hallucinations, Sound, Vibe, Immersion.
- **Logic:** `src/js/mesmer/` (Visuals, Audio).
- **UI:** _Immersive Overlays, Glitch Effects, Image Galleries._

## ⚒️ The Artificer

- **Role:** The Engineer. Structure, Layout, Components, Building Blocks.
- **Logic:** _None (Pure UI Logic)._
- **UI:** _Windows, Buttons, Grids, Panels (The "Physical" matter)._
