# RPGlitch Product Guidelines

## UX Philosophy
**Complex & Rich**: The interface should be immersive and feature-rich, offering deep interactions that support the robust underlying simulation. While maintaining offline-first local persistence, the focus is on providing a comprehensive tabletop-like experience.

## Prose & Narrative Style
**Evocative & Atmospheric**: The AI's narrative output should prioritize rich descriptions and atmospheric details, drawing the player deeply into the game world.

## Branding & Voice
**Collaborative**: The engine itself should present a helpful, encouraging, and collaborative tone, guiding the player through the roleplay experience as a supportive co-author.
