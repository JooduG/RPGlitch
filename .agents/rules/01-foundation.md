# Initial Concept
A next-generation, local-first AI Roleplay Engine built on the Perchance platform, featuring a simulation-driven architecture for deep, coherent roleplay.

# RPGlitch Product Guide

## Target Audience
**Solo RPG Players**: RPGlitch is designed for players seeking deep, immersive, single-player tabletop experiences within their browser.

## AI Narrative Role
**Collaborative Storytelling**: The AI Game Master functions as a co-author, dynamically adapting to the user's narrative direction rather than simply enforcing strict physics or adversarial challenges.

## Technical Priorities
**UI/Simulation Separation**: A critical focus is maintaining the parallel AI_TURN and USER_TURN architecture, ensuring that the interface remains unlocked and responsive while the underlying simulation calculates social dynamics and world physics in the background.

## Core Differentiator
By divorcing narrative state from the user interface and operating in discrete parallel rounds, RPGlitch provides a tabletop-like experience that supersedes standard conversational AI chatbot patterns.
