# RPGlitch Aesthetics & Sensory Guide

## Visual Identity: The Nordic Collection
The project adheres to the **Nordic Collection** aesthetic: a cool, clinical, and subterranean visual language.

### Palette: The Chalk Regime
- **Primary (Chalk)**: #FFFFFF - High-contrast, stark, and clean. Used for text and primary UI accents.
- **Secondary (Gunmetal)**: #2B2B2B - Deep, muted, and architectural. Used for backgrounds and structural elements.
- **Accent (Glitch Blue)**: #00BFFF - Low-frequency pulse for active states and critical signals.

## Image Generation Style
- **Model**: Perchance / Stable Diffusion
- **Prompting Strategy**: 'Chalk-Regime style, nordic minimalism, high-fidelity technical line-art, stark contrast, cinematic gunmetal atmosphere.'
- **Negative Prompts**: 'Vibrant colors, messy gradients, rounded corners, organic chaos.'

## Sensory Experience
- **Motion**: Kinetic, physics-based transitions. No 'bouncy' animations; only direct, meaningful displacement.
- **Audio**: Low-frequency ambient drones and sharp, clinical SFX (clicks/beeps).
