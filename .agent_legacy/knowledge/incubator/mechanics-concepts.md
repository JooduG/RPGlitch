# 🧪 Incubator: Experimental Mechanics Concept

Experimental systems for narrative resolution and branching.

## 🎲 1. The Entropy Gambit (Blackjack)

A high-stakes mini-game to resolve intense physical or social conflicts.

- **Blackjack Logic:** The user and NPC draw cards. Success (High W) grants a "Visceral" outcome. Failure (Low W) grants a "Backfire."
- **Outcome Matrix:** The result translates immediately into the next story beat.

## 🔮 2. Fate Branching (The Tarot Triad)

A system of generating three divergent choices based on Tarot archetypes:

- **The High Path (Construction):** Star, Sun, Judgement.
- **The Low Path (Destruction):** Tower, Devil, Death.
- **The Middle Path (Stasis):** Hanged Man, Moon.

## 🔥 3. Tri-Modal Physics (NSFW/Violence)

Speculative "High Entropy" modes for unrestricted realism:

- **感官过载 (Overload):** Visceral specificity.
- **写实主义 (Realism):** Physical stamina/limits.
- **背德 (Transgression):** Power exchange and psychological intensity.

## 🎲 4. The Entropy Engine (Chaos & Risk)

Outcome is never guaranteed. Every high-stakes action generates a **Chaos Seed (1-100)**.

- **Outcome Matrix:**
    - `01-15`: **BACKFIRE** (Failure + New Threat)
    - `16-40`: **FRICTION** (Struggle, Resistance, Clumsiness)
    - `41-75`: **STANDARD** (Competent success, Flow state)
    - `76-95`: **VISCERAL** (High impact, Brutality, Deep connection)
    - `96-100`: **SHATTER** (Permanent change, Death, Mind-break)
- **Fail Forward:** A "Failure" does not stop the plot; it changes the _cost_ of the plot.

## 🧱 5. Object Permanence (Causality)

The world respects **Chekhov’s Gun**. Once a variable is introduced, it must be tracked until resolution.

- **Anchor Items:** If a character draws a gun, it is in their hand until they explicitly holster it.
- **Wound Persistence:** Injuries do not heal at the end of a scene. They apply **Physiological Penalties** (Speed ↓, Focus ↓) until treated.
- **Atmospheric Mirror:** The environment is a character. If a lamp is broken, the room remains dark in subsequent turns.
