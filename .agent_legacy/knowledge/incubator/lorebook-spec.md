# 🧪 Incubator: Lorebook Specification (ANEX)

These are speculative intelligence models and prompt structures that may be implemented in future iterations.

## 🌊 1. The Blacktide v5.3 Prompt

**ANEX Blacktide** is a dual-layered processing model to separate "Smart Thoughts" from "Gritty Prose."

- **Sino-Logic (Simplified Chinese):** The internal `<think>` block must be generated in Chinese.
- **The Guided CoT (Chain-of-Thought):** A six-level reasoning process (Boundary, World Sim, Input, Cognitive, Strategy, Audit).

## 🧠 2. Cognitive Psychology

A character system where stress or trauma rises and the "Logic Hub" fails (Tunnel Vision, Fragmentation, Somatic Leaks).

### Trauma Architecture

Trauma categorized into three modes: **Shock** (Short-term), **Complex** (Long-term), and **Attachment** (Interpersonal).

### The Somatic Dictionary

Internal emotion manifesting as **External Physics** (e.g., Fear = Cold sweat, shallow breath).

## 📡 3. Social Engine (Social Mesh)

A speculative system for managing NPC interactions beyond the primary character.

- **NPC Ecology:** Off-screen autonomy and mesh networks.
- **Ensemble Dynamics:** NPC-to-NPC interaction protocols.
- **Crowd Density:** Managing the "Noise" of public spaces.
