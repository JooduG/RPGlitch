## 🗺️ The Roadmap

### Phase 1: The "Living World" (Autonomous Agency)

_Current State:_ Worker handles updates only on user message.
_Future State:_ **The "Heartbeat" runs on a timer.**

- **Idle Dreaming:** If the user hasn't replied in 5 minutes, the AI triggers an internal "thought loop" (using `worker.js`). It creates a private diary entry, or updates its own mood.
- **Unprompted DMs:** Hook the `Narcissism` engine into the Notification API. If `Narcissism > 90` and `TimeSinceLastMessage > 1h`, the AI sends a selfie or a "u up?" text.
- **Fractal Weather:** The `Fractal` entity gets its own update loop. "Entropy" rises over time. If it hits 100%, a "World Event" happens (e.g., "The cafe burns down"), forcing the story to shift.

### Phase 2: "Deep Memory" (Knowledge Graph)

_Current State:_ A linear log of messages + an "Archivist" summarizer.
_Future State:_ **RAG-powered Graph Database.**

- **Holistic Recall:** Instead of just summarizing past text, we extract _facts_ ("User hates coffee", "AI is afraid of spiders") and store them in a vector-lite structure (or just a better JSON graph).
- **Contextual Fetching:** When mentioned, pull specific facts. If the user mentions "Spiders," the AI effectively remembers its fear _viscerally_.

### Phase 3: "Memetic Evolution" (Self-Modifying Code)

_Current State:_ `RPGlitch-left-panel.txt` is static DNA.
_Future State:_ **The AI writes its own config.**

- **Dynamic Lists:** If the story becomes a "Cyberpunk Thriller", the AI _injects_ "Neon", "Rain", and "Chrome" into the `rpgMood` and `rpgStyle` lists in real-time.
- **Style Drift:** The interface itself (CSS variables) shifts based on `Entropy`. High Entropy = Glitchier fonts, erratic colors. Low Entropy = Minimalist, calm design.

### Phase 4: "Voice of God" (Audio Integration)

_Current State:_ Text + Image.
_Future State:_ **Full Sensory IO.**

- **Ambient Soundscapes:** The "Fractal" generates a prompt for an audio loop (rain, cafe noise) based on the scene descriptions.
