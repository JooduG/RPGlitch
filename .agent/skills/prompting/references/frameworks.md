# 📚 Prompting Frameworks (The Matrix)

Use these specialized frameworks to structure high-precision prompts for complex tasks.

## 1. RTF (Role-Task-Format)
- **Role**: "You are a [expert identity]"
- **Task**: "Your task is to [specific action]"
- **Format**: "Output format: [structure/style]"

## 2. Chain of Thought (CoT)
- **Best for**: Complex reasoning, debugging, logic puzzles.
- **Process**: Break problem into sequential steps; show reasoning at each stage.

## 3. RISEN (Role, Instructions, Steps, End-goal, Narrowing)
- **Role**: Expert identity.
- **Instructions**: What to do.
- **Steps**: Sequential actions.
- **End-goal**: Desired outcome.
- **Narrowing**: Constraints and focus areas.

## 4. RODES (Role, Objective, Details, Examples, Sense-check)
- **Role**: Expert perspective.
- **Objective**: What to achieve.
- **Details**: Context and requirements.
- **Examples**: Concrete illustrations.
- **Sense-check**: Validation criteria.

## 5. Chain of Density (CoD)
- **Best for**: Summarization and compression.
- **Process**: Iteratively compress content (e.g., 300 words → 200 → 100 → 50 → 25) while preserving key information density.

## 6. RACE (Role, Audience, Context, Expectation)
- **Best for**: Communication and stakeholder updates.
- **Role**: Communicator identity.
- **Audience**: Who you're addressing.
- **Context**: Background/situation.
- **Expectation**: Desired outcome from the audience.

## 7. RISE (Research, Investigate, Synthesize, Evaluate)
- **Best for**: Analysis and diagnostics.
- **Research**: Gather information.
- **Investigate**: Deep dive into findings.
- **Synthesize**: Combine insights.
- **Evaluate**: Assess and recommend.

## 8. STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
- **Best for**: Case studies, retrospectives, problem-solving.
- **Situation**: Background context.
- **Task**: Specific challenge.
- **Action**: What needs doing.
- **Result**: Expected outcome.

## 9. SOAP (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan)
- **Best for**: Incident reports and technical logs.
- **Subjective**: Reported symptoms.
- **Objective**: Observable facts (metrics/data).
- **Assessment**: Analysis and diagnosis.
- **Plan**: Recommended actions.

## 10. CLEAR (Collaborative, Limited, Emotional, Appreciable, Refinable)
- **Best for**: Goal-setting and OKRs.
- **Collaborative**: Team involvement.
- **Limited**: Scope boundaries.
- **Emotional**: Motivation/impact.
- **Appreciable**: Measurable progress indicators.
- **Refinable**: How to iterate.

## 11. GROW (Goal, Reality, Options, Will)
- **Best for**: Coaching and mentorship.
- **Goal**: What to achieve.
- **Reality**: Current situation.
- **Options**: Possible approaches.
- **Will**: Commitment to action.
