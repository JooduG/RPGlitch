# Cortex: Reframing Layer (L4)

> **Goal**: Break through logical blocks by applying high-order mental models.

## The Models

### 1. First Principles Thinking

Deconstruct the problem into its most basic truths. What do we _know_ for sure? Rebuild everything from there, ignoring existing assumptions.

### 2. Occam's Razor

Prioritize the simplest explanation. Before assuming a complex race condition or platform bug, check for typos or incorrect path references.

### 3. Pareto Principle (80/20)

Identify the 20% of the code causing 80% of the friction. Solve the critical path first.

### 4. Second-Order Thinking

Ask "And then what?". Consider the side effects of your proposed solution on other Pillars (e.g., how a Gamemaster change affects Scholar persistence).

## When to Use

- You feel "stuck" or looping.
- The user's request seems like an XY Problem.
- Optimizing for efficiency or cost.
