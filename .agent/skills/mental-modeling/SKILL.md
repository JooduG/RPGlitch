# 👓 Skill: Mental Modeling

> **The High Ground:** Reframing the problem logic.

## 1. When to Use

- **Complexity:** L4 (Reframe).
- **Trigger:** You are stuck, confused, or suspect an XY Problem.
- **Goal:** Solve the _right_ problem, not just the stated problem.

## 2. The Models

Apply one of these lenses to the problem:

### A. First Principles

- **question:** "What is actually true vs. what is assumed?"
- **action:** Deconstruct the system to its basic truths and rebuild the logic.

### B. Occam’s Razor

- **question:** "What is the simplest explanation?"
- **action:** If you have a complex theory for a bug, look for a simple typo or state error first.

### C. The Pareto Principle (80/20)

- **question:** "What 20% of work gives 80% of the result?"
- **action:** Focus on the critical path. Ignore edge cases until the core works.

### D. Second-Order Thinking

- **question:** "And then what?"
- **action:** Consider the consequences of your proposed solution.
