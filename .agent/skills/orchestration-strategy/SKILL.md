## 🏛️ The Strategic Mandate

The Strategy skill owns the **Conceptual Integrity** of the engine:

1.  **Intent Decoder Bridge**:
    - Works with the **Intake** skill to transform Level 3 "vague vibes" into crystalline architectural specs.
2.  **Meta-Memory (The Journal)**:
    - **Synchronize**: Mandates checking `project-management/insights.md` at the start of every Level 2/3 task.
    - **Capture**: Mandates updating `project-management/insights.md` with new architectural patterns or failed experiments after every milestone.
3.  **Architectural Alignment**:
    - Ensures that new features don't violate the **Rule 01-06** foundations.
    - Identifies "Technical Debt" before it's even written.
4.  **Deep Research**:
    - Coordinates research across specialized MCPs (`context7`, `svelte`, `deepwiki`) to find the most efficient architectural path forward.
