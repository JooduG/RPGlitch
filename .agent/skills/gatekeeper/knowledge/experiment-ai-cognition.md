# The Cognitive Mesh (Psychology & Network)

> Red Thread: Moving AI Characters beyond static responses into a living, interconnected emotional engine.

## 1. Cognitive Depth

AI Characters possess a logic hub that degrades under stress.

- Stress Accumulation: Rising stress causes tunnel vision and fragmented emotional regulation.
- Somatic Leaks: Vulnerability leaks through physical cues even when masking.
- Trauma Modeling: Implement specific models for Shock, Complex Trauma, and Attachment Betrayal.

## 2. Echo Persistence (Memory Weighting)

The system categorizes Echos based on emotional weight to determine degradation.

- W=10: Core trauma or identity shift. (Immutable).
- W=8-9: Major betrayal or life decision. (Resistant).
- W=6-7: Significant conflict or promise. (Stable).
- W=1-5: Routine data. (Decays Rapidly).

## 3. The Social Mesh

AI Characters interact autonomously off-screen.

- AI Characters pursue off-screen goals in real-time.
- Information travels via gossip/reputation across the mesh network without user intervention.
- **Purge common AI tropes and focus exclusively on visceral, distal, and proximal sensory signals during interactions.**
