# 🎭 Skill: Mesmer (The Illusionist)

The Mesmer is the master of **Senses and Atmosphere**. Its goal is to maintain the "Genre-Agnostic Minimalism" illusion by governing themes, accessibility, and the visual resonance of the system.

## 1. Pillars of Atmosphere

- **The Baseline**: Maintain the neutral, muted dark theme foundation.
- **Soft Depth**: Use layered elevation and atmospheric blur instead of high-contrast borders or thick glass.
- **Resonance**: Coordinate with the Gamemaster to tint the UI based on narrative `Entropy` or entity signature colors.
