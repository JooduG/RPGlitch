# 🧪 Lab 01: Ideation (The Maybe Pile)

> **Red Thread:** Before a concept becomes code, it exists here as a raw node of potential. This is the experimental horizon for RPGlitch.

## 🏮 Speculative Vision

High-level goals that steer the long-term conceptual direction of the project.

- **Multiverse Branching:** A visual node graph allowing users to explore and visualize divergent narrative paths.
- **Glitch Protocol:** Narrative events that break the fourth wall, leveraging the "Glitch" aesthetic as a storytelling device.

## 🃏 Narrative Mechanics

Speculative ways to resolve conflict and drive choice.

- **Fate Branching (The Tarot Triad):** Divergent choices generated based on Tarot archetypes (Star/Sun/Judgement for construction, Tower/Devil/Death for destruction).
- **The Entropy Gambit:** A blackjack-style mini-game for resolving high-stakes social or physical clashes.
- **Lorebook (ANEX):** A structured RAG-driven intelligence system for deep, consistent world-building.

## ⚛️ System Hypotheses

Theoretical foundations for the simulation engine.

- **Tri-Modal Physics:** Switching between 'Overload' (visceral), 'Realism' (physical limits), and 'Transgression' (psychological intensity).
- **Social Mesh:** Autonomous NPC-to-NPC relationship simulation that continues even when the user isn't present.
- **Atmospheric Mirror:** Environment damage that persists and reflects the emotional state of the narrative.
