## 🧪 Experimental Mechanics

### 1. The Entropy Gambit (Blackjack)

A high-stakes mini-game to resolve intense physical or social conflicts.

- **Logic:** User vs NPC card draw.
- **Outcomes:** High Win (Visceral) vs Low Win (Backfire).

### 2. Fate Branching (The Tarot Triad)

Generates three divergent choices based on Tarot archetypes:

- **Construction:** Star, Sun, Judgement.
- **Destruction:** Tower, Devil, Death.
- **Stasis:** Hanged Man, Moon.

### 3. Tri-Modal Physics

Speculative "High Entropy" modes for unrestricted realism:

- **Overload:** Visceral specificity.
- **Realism:** Physical limits.
- **Transgression:** Psychological intensity.

### 4. The Entropy Engine (Chaos & Risk)

Randomizer where every high-stakes action generates a **Chaos Seed (1-100)**.

- **Fail Forward:** Failure does not stop the plot, it changes the _cost_.
- **Ranges:** Backfire (1-15) to Shatter (96-100).

### 5. Object Permanence (Causality)

- **Anchor Items:** Items (e.g., guns) remain held until explicitly holstered.
- **Wound Persistence:** Injuries apply penalties until treated.
- **Atmospheric Mirror:** Environment damage persists.
