# [/10x](./idea-generation.md) - High-Leverage Strategic Idea Generation

## Objectives: Phase STRAT - Scaling

- Identify product-market fit for RPGlitch within the Perchance ecosystem.
- Design features that provide exponential (10x) value for the end-user.

## Context-Injection: Market Intelligence

- [Simulation](../rules/02-simulation.md)
- [Intelligence](../rules/05-intelligence.md)
- [Intake](../skills/intake/)

## Capabilities: Strategic Thinking

- Product Vision: Thinking like a founder to identify underserved needs.
- Architectural Audacity: Proposing structural shifts to enable 10x gains.

## Procedure

### Phase 1: Landscape Scan (Step 1.5: Intent Decoding)

1. **Market Review**: Evaluate current top-tier simulations on Perchance. Identify where RPGlitch is falling behind or could potentially lead.
2. **Gap Analysis**: Identify "impossible" features that the competitive landscape currently lacks.

### Phase 2: Ideation (Step 2: Hypothesis)

1. **Moonshot Generation**: Propose 3-5 game-changing features. Rank them by technical feasibility and market impact.
2. **Strategic Pivot**: If the current engine roadmap is too incremental, propose a "10x Pivot" to the user.

### Phase 3: Validation (Step 3: Research)

1. **Feasibility Prototype**: Conduct a research phase to verify that the proposed moonshot can be built within Perchance constraints.
2. **Proposition**: Present the 10x Strategic Plan for user authorization.

## Anti-Patterns

- **Incrementalism**: Focusing on small tweaks instead of big leaps.
- **Vibe Blindness**: Failing to account for actual user feedback or market trends.
- **Scope Creep**: Adding many small features that don't lead to a 10x outcome.
