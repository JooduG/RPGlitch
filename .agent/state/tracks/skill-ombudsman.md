## The Spec (The Meta-Optimizer)

The `ombudsman` is the interface between the agent's current task and its underlying "Operating System" (`.agent/`). It is responsible for the **Boy Scout Rule** applied to the agent's own intelligence and documentation.

- **Infrastructure Governance**: Oversees the health and hierarchy of `rules/`, `skills/`, `workflows/`, and `state/`.
- **Meta-Optimization**: Proactively refines instructions and rules to reduce cognitive friction and prevent "Cognitive Rot" in the Sovereign Core.
- **Workflow Consolidation**: Inherits and optimizes the "Paperwork Routine" from `workshop-scribe` and `project`, ensuring high-fidelity handoffs via `next-prompt.md`.
- **Markdown Authority**: Sets and enforces the `markdown` architectural standards across all agentic output.
