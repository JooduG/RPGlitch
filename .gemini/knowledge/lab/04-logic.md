# 🧠 Lab 04: Logic (Simulation Depth)

> **Red Thread:** Moving beyond static responses into a living, breathing emotional and social engine.

## 💭 Cognitive & Psychological Depth

**Hypothesis:** NPCs should possess a "Logic Hub" that fails under stress, leading to non-linear recovery and somatic leaks.

- **Stress Accumulation:** As stress rises, focus narrows (Tunnel Vision), and emotional regulation fragments.
- **Masking & Tells:** Vulnerability leaks through cues (voice cracks, fidgeting) even when an NPC tries to hide it.
- **Trauma Modeling:** Shock (flashbacks), Complex (dissociation), and Attachment (betrayal) models affect long-term behavior.

## 🕸️ The Social Mesh

**Hypothesis:** NPCs must interact with each other, not just the user, to break the "Hub-and-Spoke" model.

- **NPC Ecology:** Every Major NPC pursues goals off-screen. If they travel, they do so in real-time.
- **Triangulation:** NPCs acknowledge EACH OTHER (glances, whispers) during interactions.
- **The Mesh Network:** Information travels. Actions in front of one NPC will eventually reach others via gossip/reputation.

## 📡 Interaction Protocols

**Hypothesis:** Signal over Fact. The simulation reacts to the _impact_ of energy, not just the text.

- **Sensory Intuition:** NPCs react to distal cues (micro-expressions, rhythm) and proximal signals (pheromones, temperature).
- **Empathy Modeling:** Analyzing user syntax (short/sharp vs. long/wandering) to predict emotional needs and mirror energy.
- **Ban List (Anti-Gravity):** Purging common AI tropes (e.g., "A testament to...", "Shiver ran down his spine") in favor of visceral specificity.
